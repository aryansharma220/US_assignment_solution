Our Story

ShopSmart AI was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make online shopping as intelligent and personalized as having your own personal shopping assistant.

In today's digital marketplace, millions of products compete for attention. We realized that what shoppers really need isn't more choices, but smarter choices. That's why we built ShopSmart AI - an intelligent platform that understands your preferences, learns from your behavior, and helps you discover products that truly match your needs and budget.

Our Mission

To democratize intelligent shopping by making AI-powered product recommendations accessible to every online shopper, helping them save time, money, and make better purchasing decisions.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted AI shopping companion, where every product recommendation feels like it came from a friend who truly understands your style, needs, and budget.

AI Recommendation Engine

Collaborative Filtering

Finds users with similar taste based on their interaction history and recommends products that similar users have enjoyed. Uses Jaccard and Cosine similarity metrics.

Content-Based Filtering

Analyzes user preferences (categories, brands, price range, tags) and matches products with similar features. Uses multi-factor scoring with weighted components.

Hybrid Algorithm

Combines both collaborative and content-based approaches with configurable weights. Automatically selects the best strategy based on user history and data availability.

AI Explanations

Uses Google's Gemini API to generate natural language explanations for each recommendation. Builds context-aware prompts with user preferences and product details. Falls back to rule-based explanations when API is unavailable.
